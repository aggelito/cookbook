steps:
  - title: Prepare the Jerk Marinade
    description: In a food processor or blender, combine the Scotch Bonnet peppers, scallions, onion, ginger, garlic, allspice, thyme, brown sugar, soy sauce, lime juice, oil, salt, and pepper. Pulse until it forms a slightly chunky paste.
  - title: Marinate the Chicken
    description: Place the chicken pieces in a large bowl or resealable plastic bag. Pour the marinade over the chicken, making sure every piece is thoroughly coated. For best results, cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, ideally overnight.
  - title: Preheat the Grill
    description: Preheat your grill to a medium-high heat (about 175°C to 200°C). If using a charcoal grill, arrange the coals for indirect cooking. If you have pimento wood chips, soak them and add them to the coals for authentic smokiness.
  - title: Grill the Chicken
    description: Place the chicken on the grill. Cook for about 35-45 minutes, turning occasionally, until the skin is charred and crispy and the internal temperature reaches 74°C. Brushing with leftover marinade during the first 20 minutes of cooking is recommended.
  - title: Rest and Serve
    description: Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for 5-10 minutes before serving. This keeps the meat juicy. Serve with rice and peas and fried plantains.
